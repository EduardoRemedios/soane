# Phase Intent Review Checklist

## Version
v1.0

## Change Log
- v1.0 (2026-03-21): Generic starter-kit checklist for the Phase Intent Purple Gate.

## Purpose

This checklist is evaluated by the Purple Gate after the Phase Intent has been through Red/Blue hardening. Answer YES or NO for each item.

## Critical (must all be YES for PASS)

C1. Phase goal is concrete and verifiable.
C2. Scope boundaries are explicit and non-overlapping.
C3. Hard constraints from the source Phase Brief are preserved without weakening.
C4. Sprint budget is stated and justified.
C5. PO authority limits are explicit.
C6. Phase-level acceptance criteria are binary.
C7. No `[INFERRED]` requirements contradict source requirements.
C8. No BLOCKING open questions remain unresolved.
C9. Out-of-scope section is non-empty and covers the most likely scope-creep vectors.

## Quality (can be NO, but must be explained)

Q1. Priority order is clear enough for the PO to make trade-offs without escalating every decision.
Q2. Sprint sequencing guidance addresses known dependencies.
Q3. Compliance, policy, or operational notes are present where the phase touches sensitive paths.
Q4. Domain areas are specific enough to bound the PO's brief-writing scope.
